Solution: Describe your research and ideation approach. What tools did you use? How did you gather feedback and iterate on early ideas?

  • Used Miro / FigJam to brainstorm across categories: Settings, Locations, Events, Enemies, Mechanics
  • Asked peers to vote on top concepts to identify strongest directions
  • Created a short concept document — early location vision, player goal, and core gameplay loop
  • Gathered visual references for mood, tone, and setting

The First Roughout

Goal

Build the playable roughout fast.

Solution: Describe how you built your first playable version.

  • Transitioned from 2D map to engine and blocked out the level using simple geometry
  • Iterated in play mode to evaluate readability, navigation, and flow
  • Added placeholders for enemies, NPCs, and loot to simulate gameplay structure

Result: What did the first roughout reveal? What needed to change?
